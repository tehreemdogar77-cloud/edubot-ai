MATHEMATICS - BASIC CONCEPTS

Chapter 1: Numbers and Operations
- Natural numbers are counting numbers: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5
- Integers include negative numbers: -3, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, 3
- BODMAS Rule: Brackets, Orders, Division, Multiplication, Addition, Subtraction
- Example: 2 + 3 x 4 = 2 + 12 = 14 (multiplication before addition)

Chapter 2: Fractions
- A fraction has a numerator (top) and denominator (bottom)
- To add fractions: make denominators equal first
- Example: 1/2 + 1/3 = 3/6 + 2/6 = 5/6

Chapter 3: Algebra Basics
- Variables are letters that represent unknown numbers (x, y, z)
- Equation: 2x + 5 = 11, solving: x = 3
- Like terms: 3x + 2x = 5x
