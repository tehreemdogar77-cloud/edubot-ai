ENGLISH - GRAMMAR AND WRITING

Chapter 1: Parts of Speech
- Noun: names a person, place, or thing (teacher, school, book)
- Verb: shows action (run, write, think)
- Adjective: describes a noun (tall, beautiful, fast)
- Adverb: describes a verb (quickly, carefully, well)

Chapter 2: Tenses
- Simple Present: I write every day
- Simple Past: I wrote yesterday
- Simple Future: I will write tomorrow
- Present Continuous: I am writing right now

Chapter 3: Essay Writing
- Introduction: state your main idea (thesis)
- Body Paragraphs: support your idea with evidence
- Conclusion: summarize and restate your main point
- Always proofread your work before submitting
