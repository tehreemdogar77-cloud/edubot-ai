EXAM PREPARATION TIPS

General Tips for All Subjects:
- Make a study timetable and follow it daily
- Study in 45-minute sessions with 10-minute breaks
- Write notes in your own words, do not just copy
- Practice past papers under timed conditions
- Get 8 hours of sleep before the exam

Mathematics Exam Tips:
- Show all working steps, even if you know the answer
- Check your calculations twice
- Attempt easier questions first

Science Exam Tips:
- Draw and label diagrams clearly
- Learn definitions word by word
- Understand the concept, do not just memorize

English Exam Tips:
- Plan your essay before writing
- Use a variety of vocabulary
- Check grammar and spelling at the end
